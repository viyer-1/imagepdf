Privacy First

Your Documents Stay Yours

Built for people who handle sensitive files — lawyers, clinicians, journalists, researchers. All processing is local, always. Open source for transparency.

No Server Uploads

Your files never touch our servers. Medical scans, legal contracts, classified docs — all processed entirely on your machine, with no network traffic.

EXIF Data Stripped Automatically

Every conversion removes GPS coordinates, device fingerprints, timestamps, and camera metadata. Clean output, every time, without asking.

Fully Offline

No internet connection required. Use it on air-gapped machines, in secure facilities, or at 35,000 feet. Your workflow, your rules.

Zero Telemetry

No analytics, no crash reports, no usage logging. We don't know what you convert — and we deliberately built it that way.
